Buckee Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7/31/2024

Data Collected

Our app does not collect any personal information. The only data we collect is the number of times a sticker is clicked within the app. This data is not linked to your identity and is used soley for analytic purposes and understanding which stickers are popular among users.

How We Use the Data

The click data for stickers is collected and stored to help us improve our app by understanding user preferences. This information is anonymized and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. The data is used exclusively for improving the app's content and user experience.
